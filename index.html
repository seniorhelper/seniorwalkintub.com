In most situations, Medicare does not pay for senior walk in tubs directly, even when they help with safety. However, there may be local programs, veterans benefits or financing options that help make the project more manageable. We cannot promise coverage, but we can share what other customers in similar situations have done.

How long does installation usually take?

Many senior walk in tub installations can be completed in about a day once everything is prepared. More complex jobs, such as moving plumbing or widening doorways, can take longer. When we review your photos and layout, we‚Äôll give you a more realistic timeline before you commit.

What if a low threshold shower would be better than a tub?
